Welcome

Our mission as a school is to create a learning environment that encourages student growth, both in academics, and as individuals. Our school pioneers new teaching strategies to help students learn their best. We encourage students to learn at their own pace, and provide a framework to help students build interest in learning.

Curriculum

Our school utilizes a project-based teaching strategy to better prepare students for the real world. Instead of daily assignments, students are tasked with working on overarching projects that encompass multiple subject areas. This strategy allows us to teach both core subject areas and other important skills such as collaboration and critical thinking simultaneously, while keeping students interested in learning.

While at Autumn Hills Academy, students are encouraged to learn about subjects such as music and the arts. Alongside our collection of courses on topics from the humanities to applied science and technology, students are also offered 1-to-1 tutoring and assistance on personal projects.

Grading

Research has shown that our traditional methods of grading do not accurately represent a student's understanding of content. At Autumn Hills Academy, we utilize a system that grades students on their understanding of concepts, not assignments. Each individual project-based assignment is broken up into multiple different concepts. Students then, if the student is not happy with their grade, they have the opportunity to return to these concept areas to prove their understanding at any point in the school year.
